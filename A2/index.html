Tourism analysis of Australia

Australia is a country with a rich history and diverse culture. It is a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year with its stunning landscapes, vibrant cities, and unique wildlife. The blend of natural wonders and cultural attractions makes it a captivating place to explore, drawing tourists from around the globe to experience its unparalleled beauty and charm.
This visualization explores the trends in visits to Australia.
Follow the journey to discover how Tourism impacts the economy in Australia, trends of tourism, and the information grouped by different categories of visitors around the world.


TOURISM CONTRIBUTION TO AUSTRALIA GROSS VALUE

The visualization shows a line representing the percentage of gross value added by TOURISM over the TOTAL gross value of Australia. It indicates a trend that tourism is contributing an increasing amount to the national gross value after year 2021 (which is believed to be due to COVID-19).

There is also bars on the graph to explicitly show the Total Gross Value added by tourism to the economy, in $billions.
